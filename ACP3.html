Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is one of the most famous monuments in the world, located in Agra, India. It was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

History

Construction of the Taj Mahal began in 1632 and was completed in 1653. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is considered one of the finest examples of Mughal architecture.

Features

  • Made of white marble
  • Intricate carvings and inlay work
  • Beautiful gardens and reflecting pool

Fun Fact

The Taj Mahal changes color depending on the time of day, appearing pinkish in the morning, white in the afternoon, and golden at night.
