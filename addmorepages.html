Chinese Cuisine

Chinese cuisine is an important part of Chinese culture, originating from various regions of China and Chinese communities worldwide. Due to historical influence, Chinese food has shaped many other Asian cuisines. Staples such as rice, soy sauce, noodles, tea, tofu, and utensils like chopsticks and the wok are now recognized globally.

Pizza

Pizza (Italian: ['pittsa], Neapolitan: ['pittsa]) is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and various ingredients (anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc.). It is traditionally baked in a wood-fired oven. A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta. A person who makes pizza is known as a pizzaiolo.
