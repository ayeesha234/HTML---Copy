About Honeybees

Honeybees are flying insects known for their role in pollination and honey production. They are social insects that live in colonies with a structured hierarchy consisting of a queen, worker bees, and drones.

Types of Honeybees

  • Queen Bee: The only fertile female in the colony responsible for laying eggs.
  • Worker Bees: Female bees that perform tasks like foraging, cleaning, and taking care of the larvae.
  • Drones: Male bees whose primary role is to mate with the queen.

Importance of Honeybees

Honeybees are crucial for agriculture as they pollinate many crops. They also produce honey and beeswax, which are valuable natural products.

Fun Facts

Did you know? A single honeybee visits around 50-100 flowers during a foraging trip!
